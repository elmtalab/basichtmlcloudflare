Awesome Features

Blockchain Based Identity

The easiest way to integrate verifiable credentials into your data workflows. PandoraID Credentials are tamper-proof through the use of cryptography. Personal Data is not stored on centralised servers.

One Identity For Everything

A secure and digital peer-to-peer channel is established between ID Issuer, ID Owner and ID Verifier. When credentials are exchanged not even PandoraID system provider knows what is being exchanged.

Scalable

PandoraID credentials is an identity metasystem that can be verified anywhere, at any time. Even if the issuer does not exist anymore. This allows to verify identity across multiple platforms and locations.

Fast Response Time

Rather than running on an individual machine Pandora functions run on Edge Network a growing global network of thousands of machines distributed across hundreds of locations.

Self-sovereign identity (SSI) is an approach to digital identity that gives individuals control of their digital identities. SSI addresses the difficulty of establishing trust in an interaction. In order to be trusted, one party in an interaction will present credentials to the other parties, and those relying parties can verify that the credentials came from an issuer that they trust. In this way, the verifier's trust in the issuer is transferred to the credential holder.
